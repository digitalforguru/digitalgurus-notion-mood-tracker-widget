FAQ

How do I add this to Notion?

Copy your embed link, go to Notion, type /embed, paste the link, and confirm!

Does it save my moods?

Yes! This mood tracker saves your mood entries so your weekly mood board stays connected.

Can I reset or view my mood log?

Yes! Use reset to clear the week or mood year to view your saved yearly mood log.
